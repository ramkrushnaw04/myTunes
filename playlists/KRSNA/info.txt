Krsna, also known as KR$NA, is an Indian rapper and music producer known for his versatile approach to music, blending various genres and producing thought-provoking lyrics. Born on April 13, 1992, in New Delhi, Krsna has played a significant role in shaping the Indian hip-hop scene. His notable works include albums like "Still Here" and "Vyanjan," and he has collaborated with prominent figures in the Indian music industry. In addition to his music career, Krsna is an entrepreneur, founding the independent record label and hip-hop collective, Kalamkaar. Recognized for his influence in Indian hip-hop, Krsna continues to contribute to the growth of the genre in the country.
